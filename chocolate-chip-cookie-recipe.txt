Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

2 sticks butter
3/4 cup white sugar
3/4 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1 t vanilla extract
1 t baking soda
1/2 t salt
2 1/4 cups white flower
2 cups chocolate chips
Lots of love

Instructions:

1.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees
2.  Cream first three ingredients together until smooth.
3.  Add eggs and vanilla.  Mix well.
4.  Add soda, salt, and flower, mixing well.
5.  Add chocolate chips
6.  Bake for about 10-15 minutes, depending on the cookie size.  Yummy!
